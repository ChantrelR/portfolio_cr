Baker. Builder. Problem solver. Here's a little more about who I am and what drives me.

My Story

I'm Chantrel Ritfeld — a Software Engineering student at UNASAT in Suriname, currently studying Programming Fundamentals. I'm passionate about building things that have a real impact, whether that's writing clean code or layering a perfect buttercream cake.

My journey into tech started with curiosity: I wanted to understand how the devices around me actually worked. That curiosity led me to Arduino, to HTML and CSS, and eventually to the broader world of software engineering. Every project I take on teaches me something new.

Outside of code, baking is where I recharge. There's a quiet precision to it — measuring, timing, adjusting — that mirrors the problem-solving mindset I bring to programming. Both worlds demand patience, creativity, and an eye for detail.
